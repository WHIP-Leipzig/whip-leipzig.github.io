## Audience
The meetup is open to everyone who agrees with and lives our [principles](/en/prinzipien.html). The soft age guideline for participants is around 40. The organizers reserve the right to filter the audience and, if needed, refer people to other meetups, to ensure a pleasant atmosphere and good experiences for everyone.

## Focus
The thematic focus of the meetup is always BDSM. The organizers try to provide each meetup with a thematic focus to encourage exchange. Ultimately, the goal is to help educate interested people and to offer a safe entry point into the topic.

## Frequency
The [WHIP meetup](/en/faq.html#what-kinds-of-meetups-are-there-at-whip) usually takes place once a month, ideally on the first Friday of the month. [Play-WHIP](/en/faq.html#what-kinds-of-meetups-are-there-at-whip) is typically planned for the second Tuesday of the month.
